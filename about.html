Our Story

Building Dreams Since 2009

Stackly was founded with a simple mission: to make luxury real estate accessible to everyone. What started as a small team of passionate real estate professionals has grown into one of the most trusted names in the industry.

Over the past 15 years, we've helped thousands of families find their perfect homes and assisted countless investors in building impressive property portfolios. Our commitment to excellence, transparency, and client satisfaction has earned us numerous awards and the trust of clients worldwide.

Today, Stackly operates in over 50 cities across the globe, with a team of 500+ expert agents dedicated to making your real estate dreams a reality.
